Boundary Disputes

← Back to our services

We help landowners establish clear boundaries on their property, especially in relation to ownership.

No-one wants a neighbour encroaching on their property, so to avoid potential boundary disputes, know for certain where your land starts and stops.
When you’re buying or selling property, it’s crucial that the property you’re buying is actually the property listed in the real estate contract. We provide detailed surveying and legal advice for property owners who may need clarification on the location of a boundary, ownership and land value.
